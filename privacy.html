Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how StatusHD Pro (“we”, “our” or “us”) handles information when you use the StatusHD Pro Android application and this website. StatusHD Pro is developed by FadSync (Abdul Wahab).

1. Overview

StatusHD Pro is designed to process videos and images on your device so they can be optimized for WhatsApp Status. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address or phone number.

2. Information processed by the app

When you select a video or image inside the app, StatusHD Pro processes your media using our proprietary enhancement engine. Parts of this processing may occur on your device or through secure cloud-assisted methods depending on what is required to generate the optimized result.

Your media is used only to perform the requested enhancement and is not used to identify you. We do not store your files after processing is completed.

3. Advertising and analytics

StatusHD Pro may display in-app advertisements using third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdMob). These services may collect certain information automatically, such as anonymous identifiers, device information or approximate location, to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. Any data collected in this way is handled directly by those third-party providers under their own privacy policies.

We may also use basic, privacy-friendly analytics (such as install counts or crash reports) to improve app stability and user experience. These analytics typically do not identify you personally.

4. Permissions

To function correctly, StatusHD Pro may request the following Android permissions:

  • Storage / Media access: to read and write videos and images you choose to process.
  • Network access: to load ads and check for updates or remote configuration.

You can manage these permissions at any time from your device settings.

5. Children’s privacy

StatusHD Pro is not specifically targeted at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete that information.

6. Third-party links

This website and the app may include links to external sites such as Instagram, GitHub or app stores. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites. Please review their own policies before sharing any information.

7. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited information we handle. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, relevant laws or our practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
